    • Research interests

    My research interests are mainly focused around Analytic Number theory, primarily studying the distribution of primes as well as in Combinatorial Number theory. I also take particular interest in obtaining fully explicit results.

    My research could be broadly classified into:

    ​
    • Sieve theory -  applications to arithmetic problems, estimating primes in cosets, refinement and applications of the large sieve inequality, etc. 
      • ​​
    • The Circle method, exponential sums over primes and their applications to problems of additive and multiplicative nature.
      • ​
    • ​ The interface of additive combinatorics and Number theory - distribution of products of primes in arithmetic progressions, applications of the Transference principle, etc.
    • Correlations of arithmetic functions, including studying shifted convolution sums and their error terms. ​
